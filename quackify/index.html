Quackify

Convert your email address into a privacy alias in one tap.

About

Quackify is a minimalist tool that converts any email address into a DuckDuckGo Email Protection privacy alias. Just enter your real email and your alias prefix, tap Convert, and instantly get your alias ready to copy.

Features

  • Instant conversion — turn any email into a privacy alias in one tap
  • One-tap copy — copy the result to your clipboard immediately
  • Clean, distraction-free interface built with SwiftUI
  • Apple Watch support — convert and copy aliases right from your wrist
  • Works offline — no account or login required

PolyDice

The dice roller that knows what game you're playing.

About

PolyDice renders your dice exactly as they look on the table — geometric shapes with authentic game symbols. Whether you're playing Mansions of Madness, HeroQuest, or rolling a stealth check on a d20, every die looks the part.

Features

  • Geometric dice rendering — d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20 drawn as actual face shapes
  • Game-aware presets — choose your game and roll the right dice automatically
  • Apple Watch support
  • Clean SwiftUI interface
  • Works offline — no account or login required
